Introduction

Predicting human morphological traits using genomic data (aka, DNA portraying or DNA phenotyping) shows promising prospect in forensic studies. To fill up the vacancy of single nucleotide polymorphism (SNP) data related to human morphological characters and to make SNP data more friendly to use, we establish a DNA portrait landscape, which is the first portrait-specific database focusing on the relationship between genes and different kinds of morphological characteristics of humans.
